Opening the facility's insurance file

Providing the service of opening the insurance file for your project; Egyptian law requires - in accordance with Article No. (3) of the Egyptian Labor Law - that all companies or individuals who practice industrial or commercial activity must open an insurance file, whether for their company, factory, institution, etc., in accordance with the principle of transferring responsibility for confronting risks. With the government’s support for entrepreneurs, obtaining the industrial register provides many incentives and advantages, including:

Obtaining an exemption from the financial compensation for employment.

Giving priority to local factories in contracting with government and private agencies, using the document as an official document
