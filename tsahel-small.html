Small And Medium Finance

  • Minimum Financing Is 200,000 EGP.
  • The Highest Financing Is 5M EGP.
  • The Minimum Age For The Financing Applicant Is 21 Years And The Maximum Is 65 Upon The Expiry Of The Last Financing Installment Payment Date.
  • The client must have an existing activity for a period of no less than six months.
  • A Guarantor Is Required.
  • Financing period and repayment systems: ranging from 3 months to 36 months (gradually). Repayment begins one month after the date of disbursement of the financing.
  • The financing value is determined according to the size of the activity and the ability to repay.

Papers And Documents:

  • Fill Out The Financing Application Form With The Signatures Of The Financing Applicant And The Guarantor.
  • Bank statement for the last 6 months.
  • A Valid National ID Card (Customer And Guarantor).
  • Provide a budget for the project or according to the amount of funding required.
  • A Proof of residence for the client, the guarantor, and the location of the activity by submitting any government document (electricity, gas, telephone, water receipt).
  • License to practice the activity, tax card, and a recent extract from the commercial registry and a recent extract from it - if any - (in case the financing amount is requested is greater than 35,000 pounds).
  • Conducting credit inquiries about customers through the company.
